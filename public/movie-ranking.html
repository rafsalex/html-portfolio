My top 3 movies of all time

Alita Battle Angel

My favourite female protaganist of all time, highly recomemnded.
Made available to all amputees with missing arms, the film producers donated prosthetic arms that look just like the lead character's in the movie! How great is that! I wholeheartedly support films that make everyone feel they are heroes even if they have missing limbs.

Avatar

One of my favourite sci-fi/fantasy type like movies where politics and cultural misunderstandning leads to war. A spin on learning about alien culture in an unkown planet, a very used cliche but in this movie, well represented.

Spirited Away

Hands down an anime classic of all time! When I think off the top of my head favourite anime movie, Spirited Away definitely makes it to the top for me.
